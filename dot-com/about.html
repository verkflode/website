Our Story: Turning a European Crisis into an Opportunity

Across Europe, millions of tonnes of perfectly good food are wasted every year. This isn't just a missed opportunity to feed vulnerable populations; it's a significant driver of climate change. Every kilogram of wasted food generates 2.5 kilograms of CO2, turning kitchens across the continent into inadvertent climate accelerators.

The problem isn't a lack of awareness, but a lack of imagination.
